Accounting

Delve into the language of business. Accounting focuses on recording, summarizing, and analyzing financial transactions. You'll learn about financial statements, budgeting, and how to make informed business decisions. Its history is rooted in ancient civilizations, evolving into the cornerstone of modern commerce.

Tutor:

Anthony Darko

Economics & Management Sciences (EMS)

Build a strong foundation for future business leaders. EMS introduces the exciting worlds of the economy, entrepreneurship, and financial literacy. This subject covers how societies manage resources and lays the groundwork for senior phase subjects like Accounting and Economics.

Tutor:

Anthony Darko

Natural Sciences

Uncover the principles that govern the natural world. This subject is the gateway to Physics, Chemistry, and Life Sciences. You'll conduct experiments, learn about matter, energy, and living systems, fostering a spirit of inquiry and discovery.

Tutor:

Anthony Darko

Mathematics

Master the universal language of problem-solving. From basic arithmetic to advanced calculus, Mathematics develops logical reasoning and abstract thinking. It is the essential tool for science, technology, engineering, and finance.

Tutor:

Anthony Darko

Mathematical Literacy

Apply mathematical concepts to everyday life. Mathematical Literacy focuses on practical skills needed for managing personal finances, interpreting data in the media, and making sense of the world around you. It’s math for the real world.

Tutor:

Anthony Darko

Engineering Graphics & Design (EGD)

Bring ideas to life through technical drawing. Engineering Graphics and Design (EGD) is the art of visual communication for technical fields. Learn freehand, instrument, and computer-aided drawing (CAD) to design the products and buildings of the future.

Tutor:

Mr. Ramson (E.G.D Educator at South City College)
